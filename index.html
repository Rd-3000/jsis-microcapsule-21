Welcome

This website presents our latest research on JSIS Microcapsule 21, focusing on efficient microencapsulation techniques for various industries including food, pharmaceuticals, and materials science.

About the Project

JSIS Microcapsule 21 is a cutting-edge research initiative aimed at developing advanced microcapsules for controlled release applications. Our team combines expertise in chemistry, materials engineering, and biotechnology.

Key Research Findings

  • Improved encapsulation efficiency up to 95%
  • Enhanced stability in extreme conditions
  • Applications in targeted drug delivery
  • Sustainable production methods using bio-based materials
